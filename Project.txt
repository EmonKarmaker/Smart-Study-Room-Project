EEE 2124 Electronics Laboratory Project
Smart Study Room 
Features :

1) It will display a message "Keep quiet" in the study room if any of the students make noise over a certain decibel 
2) A lamp will be automatically on if a person seats to study (Use sensors)
3) At the door there will be a display that shows the status of the study room ( total capacity, remaining capacity, temperature of the room) 
4) If the temperature of the room crossed a fixed level fan / ac will be on automatically. ( Use a demo fan for implementation )
